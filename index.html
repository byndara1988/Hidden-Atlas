Hi, I'm Kat 👋

Travel Creator · Tourism Graduate · Explorer

I run The Hidden Atlas — a travel blog and YouTube channel born out of a genuine passion for exploring the world. I studied tourism and I truly believe that travel doesn't have to be expensive to be extraordinary.

I love diving into local cultures, collecting fascinating geographical facts and finding places well beyond the tourist trail. I've travelled across Africa, the Middle East, Europe, the Americas and everywhere in between.

My mission is simple: inspire you to travel on a budget, show you the tricks that make it possible, and make sure you do it safely — because knowledge is the best protection when you're on the road.
