Travel Knowledge

Smarter Travel Starts Here

A few things I wish someone had told me before my first big trip.

💱

Never Exchange at the Airport

Airport currency exchange booths charge up to 15% in hidden fees. Use a Revolut card or withdraw from a local ATM — you'll save a surprising amount on every trip.

🗺️

Download Offline Maps Before You Go

Maps.me works with zero internet connection. Download your destination city before you board the plane — it's saved me in places with no signal more times than I can count.

🏨

Book First & Last Night Only

Lock in your arrival and departure accommodation, but leave the middle flexible. The best travel moments happen when you follow the locals' advice on the ground.

🎒

Pack Half of What You Think You Need

A lighter bag means faster movement, lower luggage fees and less stress. You can almost always buy what you forgot — and locals' markets beat tourist shops every time.

🌍

Research Local Customs First

Dress codes, tipping culture, greeting rituals — a little research before you arrive turns awkward moments into genuine connections. It's the single best investment of 20 minutes.

🔒

Split Your Money Across 3 Places

Card in your bag, small cash in your pocket, emergency note hidden in your luggage. Petty theft happens — it doesn't have to ruin your trip when you're prepared.
