Digital Products — No Refunds After Download

All products sold through The Hidden Atlas are digital files (e-books, spreadsheets, travel planners). Because digital files can be copied and retained once downloaded, I am unable to offer refunds once a file has been accessed or downloaded.

This is consistent with the EU Consumer Rights Directive, which provides that consumers lose the right of withdrawal for digital content once the download has begun with their consent.

Exceptions — I'll Always Do Right by You

If any of the following apply, please contact me and I will resolve the issue:

  • The file is corrupted or does not open
  • You received the wrong file
  • There is a significant error in the content

In these cases, I will send you a corrected file or, at my discretion, issue a full refund. Just get in touch — I want you to be happy with your purchase.

Before You Purchase

Please read the full product description carefully and check the file format listed (e.g. .xlsx, .pdf) before purchasing to make sure it works on your device.

If you have any questions before buying, feel free to message me — I'm happy to help.
